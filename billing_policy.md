# Billing and Refund Policy

## Subscription Plans
- Free: 0 dollars/month — 1 user, 100 API calls/day
- Pro: 49 dollars/month — 5 users, 10,000 API calls/day
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — unlimited users and API calls

## Billing Cycle
- Subscriptions renew automatically on the same date each month
- You will receive an invoice email 3 days before renewal
- Payment methods accepted: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, bank transfer (Enterprise only)

## Refund Policy
- Monthly plans: Refund available within 7 days of charge if unused
- Annual plans: Pro-rated refund within 30 days of purchase
- No refunds after service has been actively used
- Disputed charges must be raised within 60 days

## How to Request a Refund
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Billing > Request Refund
3. Select the charge and provide a reason
4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method

## To Cancel Subscription
Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan. Your access continues until end of billing period.
