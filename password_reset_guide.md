# Password Reset Guide

## Overview
Users can reset their password using the self-service portal or by contacting support.

## Steps to Reset Password
1. Go to https://app.example.com/forgot-password
2. Enter your registered email address and click 'Send Reset Link'
3. Check your inbox for an email from noreply@example.com (check spam if not received)
4. Click the reset link within 30 minutes — links expire after 30 minutes
5. Enter your new password (min 8 characters, must include one uppercase, one number)
6. Click 'Save Password' and log in with your new credentials

## Account Locked After Failed Attempts
If your account is locked after 5 failed login attempts:
- Wait 15 minutes for automatic unlock
- Or contact support at support@example.com with your account email

## Common Issues
- Reset email not received: Check spam folder or verify the email address is correct
- Link expired: Request a new reset link from the portal
- Still cannot log in: Your account may be suspended — contact billing support
