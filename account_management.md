# Account Management Guide

## Creating an Account
1. Visit https://app.example.com/signup
2. Enter your work email and create a password
3. Verify your email address via the confirmation link
4. Complete your profile and select a plan

## Updating Account Details
- Name and email: Settings > Profile > Edit
- Password: Settings > Security > Change Password
- Two-factor authentication: Settings > Security > Enable 2FA (recommended)

## Account Suspension
Accounts may be suspended for:
- Non-payment after 14 days past due date
- Violation of Terms of Service
- Suspected fraudulent activity

To reactivate a suspended account:
1. Contact support@example.com with subject: Account Reactivation Request
2. Provide your account email and reason for suspension
3. If due to payment, settle the outstanding balance via the payment link sent to your email
4. Reactivation typically takes 1-2 business days

## Deleting Your Account
Account deletion is permanent. To delete:
- Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
- All data will be permanently removed within 30 days per our data retention policy
- Export your data before deletion via Settings > Data Export
