# Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

## Uptime Commitment
- Pro Plan: 99.9% monthly uptime (max 43 minutes downtime/month)
- Enterprise Plan: 99.99% monthly uptime (max 4 minutes downtime/month)
- Free Plan: No uptime guarantee

## Incident Response Times
| Severity | Description | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|----------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|
| P1 Critical | Full outage | 15 minutes | 2 hours |
| P2 High | Major feature broken | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| P3 Medium | Minor feature issue | 4 hours | 48 hours |
| P4 Low | Cosmetic or question | 24 hours | 1 week |

## SLA Credits
If uptime falls below commitment, you are eligible for service credits:
- 99.0-99.9% uptime: 10% credit on monthly bill
- 95.0-99.0% uptime: 25% credit
- Below 95%: 50% credit

To claim SLA credits, submit a request within 30 days of the incident with the incident ID from status.example.com

## Scheduled Maintenance
Maintenance windows are every Sunday 02:00-04:00 UTC and do not count toward downtime.
